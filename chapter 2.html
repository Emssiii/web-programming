Breaking Bad

A critically acclaimed crime drama about a chemistry teacher turned meth kingpin.

Plot

Walter White, a struggling high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with cancer, partners with a former student, Jesse Pinkman, to manufacture and sell methamphetamine, leading to unforeseen consequences.
