Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Simple yet delicious!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta (or bacon), diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 clove garlic (optional)

Method

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add garlic for extra flavor, then remove it.
  3. Whisk eggs with cheese and black pepper in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta and mix it with the pancetta, removing the pan from heat.
  5. Quickly mix in the egg mixture, stirring constantly to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.
